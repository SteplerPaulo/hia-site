RAISON D’ETRE

Reason for existence

VISION
A family who lives the TRUTH IN LOVE, is actively involved in building Christian communities, in dialogue of life with the poor and in caring for Mother Earth.

MISSION
We, the members of the HIA Family, animated by the Trinitarian Spirituality and Apostolic Vicariate of Calapan pastoral thrust, commit ourselves to the task

GOALS
Animated by the Trinitarian Spirituality and the Apostolic Vicariate of Calapan pastoral thrust, we strive to

  1. develop the total person through a relevant and quality education rooted in Christian values;
  2. make Christ and His values especially simplicity of life permeate our programs policies and activities;
  3. promote and uphold the dignity of the human person and make a stand for truth, justice and freedom;
  4. prepare the person for responsible leadership and selfless service;
  5. participate in the strengthening of families and Basic Communities through the Hapag ng Pamilyang Mindoreño;
  6. bring ourselves and others closer to God by our dialogue of life with the poor in the community especially among indigenous people, women, children and youth;
  7. foster love of country and promote, preserve and develop our cultural heritage; and
  8. care for and preserve of Mother Earth.

CORE VALUES

  • LOVING SERVICE
  • INTEGRITY
  • FAITH
  • EXCELLENCE

ENROLLMENT

ENROLLMENT PROCEDURE
Enrolment serves as a vital part in the life of every student/pupil upon entering schools, either public or in private. It gives the students/pupils the impression on how the whole institute manages and manipulates the entire body. Moreover, enrolment serves as the first experience of all students/pupils by which he/she takes part either individually or with company of a parent or some relatives.

  • PRESCHOOL
    • NURSERY (3 years old by October)
    • KINDER 1 (4 years old by October)
    • KINDER 2 (5 years old by October)
  • GRADE SCHOOL
    • Special Science Class (Grade 1-6)
  • HIGH SCHOOL
    • Special Science Class (I-IV)

PRE-ENROLLMENT
Pre-enrollment is done every February of the current year to check the outstanding population and serves as preparation for the next school year. Students from Preschool / Grade Six who wish to enroll are given registration form to fill up. Registration forms are also given to other students who are interested to enroll during the Campaign Period.

ENROLLMENT PROCESS

FOR PRESCHOOL/GRADE ONE/FRESHMEN/TRANFEREES

  • STEP 1
    • Proceed to INTERVIEW ROOM for interview and evaluation of documents
  • STEP 2
    • Fill up Enrolment /Assessment Form at ROOM 1.
  • STEP 3
    • Go to Station 2 for the Assessment of payment
  • STEP 4
    • Pay at the Accounting office
    • Secure an Admissions Slip
FOR OLD STUDENTS
  • STEP 1
    • Fill up Enrolment /Assessment Form at ROOM 1. Submit the old report card.
  • STEP 2
    • Go to Station 2 for the Assessment of payment
  • STEP 3
    • Pay at the Accounting office
    • Secure an Admissions Slip
